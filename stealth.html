Shadow Steps

Swift Strike

Assassinate

Thief

Lock Picking

You may pick non-magical locks with lockpicking tools, with advantage.

Enhanced Hearing

You may hear conversations and footsteps through doors.

Sleight of Hand

Gain advantage on dexterity checks.

Shift

Twice per day, you may phase through any object, weapon or creature.

Scout

Concealed in Darkness

While you are in darkness and not in combat, you cannot be seen by anyone in a 30FT radius.

Tripwire

You may place a tripwire between two objects. This tripwire cannot be seen with non-magical sight. If a creature unknowingly walks into the tripwire, they are Tripped, immediately stopping their movement for the turn, alerting you to their location and making the next attack against the creature a Sneak Attack. This ability costs 1 CP in combat.

Concealing Shroud

Spend 1 CP to unleash a shroud in a 30FT radius which lasts 1 turn. Only you may see through this shroud. This ability also immediately puts the user into Stealth.

Ethereal Servant

Spend 2 CP to create an Ethereal Servant. The servant may only travel within a 100FT radius of you and is immediately destroyed when hit. The servant may carry out a basic action or attack, and its attacks may activate any bonuses that its user has unlocked. After carrying out its action or attack, it is destroyed. When destroyed, the servant erupts into a poisonous mist, applying Poisoned to all creatures in a 10FT radius.

Shadow Cloak

At the start of your turns you may choose to enter stealth.
