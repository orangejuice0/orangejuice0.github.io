Blunt Weapons

Whip

Lasso

You may grab objects and creatures within the range of your whip. If a creature is targeted by Lasso, it is afflicted with Restrict. If an object is targeted by Lasso, you may fling it a maximum of 30FT.

Lacerate

Your whip attacks now applies Bleeding.

Puppeteering

If you have captured a creature with Lasso, you may spend 1 CP - It performs a melee attack with its stats on a target within its range. The attacking creature has disadvantage on its damage roll.

Whirl

If you have captured a creature with Lasso, you may spend X CP (where X is your current CP) - deal damage proportional to its Max HP to all targets within a 10FT radius X times.

Hammer

Crush

Your first hammer attack each turn applies damage modifiers twice.

Wind-Up

A You may spend 1 CP to gain a stack of Wind-Up. Your next attack deals an extra 1D6 damage per stack of Wind-Up. At two stacks of Wind-Up, your next attack applies Dazed to its target until the start of its next turn.

Overflowing Strength

Gauntlet

Ambidexterity

Your follow-up attacks now deal 1D6 base damage

Crescendo

When you damage an enemy with a gauntlet, you gain +1 damage on all future gauntlet attacks that turn. If you fail an attack, Crescendo is reset.

Expunge

Once per turn, you may spend 1 CP - perform a gauntlet attack. This attack instead deals 1D10 base damage, does not apply strength modifiers, and applies the Crescendo modifier thrice. This attack does not activate follow-up attacks.

Relentless

Your follow-up attacks now activate follow-up attacks. These additional follow-up attacks suffer disadvantage on attack rolls.

Concussion

Once per combat, you may choose to enhance an attack. This attack applies 1 turn of Concussion.
