Performance

Dance

Rejuvenating Waltz

At the start of your turns, once per combat, you may choose to spend all of your CP. Gain 5FT movement for each CP spent this way until either the combat or Rejuvenating Waltz ends. While Rejuvenating Waltz is active, you may no longer damage enemies. Targeting allies with attacks heals them for half the damage the attack would deal. Rejuvenating Waltz ends if you do not perform a movement action on your turn.

Rising Tension

At the end of each of your turns, increase the movement of all battling allied creatures by 5FT for the duration of the combat. This effect is removed when Rejuvenating Waltz ends. If multiple allies have this ability, it only activates once.

Dramatic Climax

After Rising Tension has activated 3 times, at the start of each of your turns, give all allies +2 to their next dice roll. This effect is removed when Rejuvenating Waltz ends. If multiple allies have this ability, it only activates once.

Grand Finale

After Dramatic Climax has activated twice, you may spend 3 CP and end your turn. Immediately end Rejuvenating Waltz and remove its associated effects. All battling enemies lose HP equal to your bonus movement from the effects of Rejuvenating Waltz and Rising Tension before it was removed. Enemies are additionally afflicted with 1 turn of Paralysis, 2 turns of Dazed and 3 turns of Entranced.

Music

Rallying Song

Before combat starts, you may spend 1 CP. All allies gain +1 to their initiative. All enemies are afflicted with -1 to their CHA.

Cursed Ballad

You may spend 2 CP - All battling enemies must make a charisma saving throw. Upon failing the saving throw, enemies are afflicted with 1 turn of Entranced and gain the following effect: Take damage equal to the amount of times you have been afflicted by ailments at the end of each of your turns.

Calming Lullaby

You may spend 2 CP - All battling enemies must make a charisma saving throw. Upon failing the saving throw, enemies are afflicted with 1 turn of Slumber and gain disadvantage on all future charisma saving throws.

Chaotic Symphony

If you have activated Rallying Song, Ballad and Lullaby this combat, you may spend 3 CP. All enemies that have failed a charisma saving throw in this combat are afflicted with 2 turns of Fear, Dazed, Bewildered, Bleeding, Burning, Frozen, Electrocuted, Poisoned, Deafened and Blinded.

Storytelling

Set the Stage

When combat begins, designate another battling ally as a Hero and a battling enemy as a Villain. The Hero gains +1 on all rolls against the Villain. If the Hero’s HP reaches 0, the Villain becomes victorious, granting it +1 to all its rolls for the rest of combat and ending all other effects of and relating to Set the Stage. If the Villain’s HP reaches 0, you may designate a new Villain. There may only be one Hero and one Villain at a time in each combat.

A Tale Told Far and Wide

Battling allies not designated as the Hero are designated as Admirers. Each time an Admirer successfully attacks the Villain, apply one of the following effects to the Hero in order: Heal 5 HP; Randomly increase one stat by 1 until the end of your next turn; Reduce the duration of an ailment you are afflicted with by 1 turn (only applies to ailments afflicted during the combat). Each Admirer may only activate one effect per round.

Triumphant Victory

If the Hero reduces the Villain’s HP to 0, all Admirers heal 10 HP. If the combat has not ended, the Hero additionally gains advantage to all rolls until the end of their next turn.

Final Stand

If the Hero’s HP would reach 0, instead heal the Hero to their Max HP and set their CP to 5. The Hero additionally gains +5 to all their rolls against the Villain. If the Villain remains alive at the end of the Hero’s next turn, reduce the Hero’s HP to 0. This effect can only activate once per combat.

The Highest Form of Flattery

Once per round, when another battling creature performs an action, you may Observe that action. On your next turn, if you are physiologically able to, you may perform the Observed action at no cost (their bonuses do not apply).
