Movement

Swiming

Swimming

Your swimming speed is now equal to your movement.

Dive

Upon entering water, your swimming speed is briefly doubled, lasting one turn.

Jet

While swimming, you may quickly propel yourself a distance equal to half your swimming speed. This ability costs 1 CP in combat.

One with Water

You may now create a current in a specified direction in any body of water than you are in. The movement of creatures swimming in the direction of the current is doubled, and the movement of creatures swimming against the direction of the current is halved.

Athleticism

Leap

You may choose to replace your movement action with a leap. This will travel the same distance as your movement.

Sprint

Once per turn, you may spend 1 CP to take an additional movement action. This action must be on land, and does not affect the movement of a vehicle or mount.

Supersonic

Sprint instead grants two additional movement actions.

Fountain of Stamina

Sprint no longer has a CP cost.

Climbing

Climbing

Your climbing speed is now equal to half your movement.

Climbing+

Your climbing speed is now equal to your movement.

Adhesive Soles

You may now climb without the use of your hands.

Cloud Steps

You may now briefly walk on air. Doing so in combat requires Focus, costs 1 CP to activate, and 1 CP per turn to maintain in following turns.

Blink

Once per round, you may teleport anywhere in a 5FT radius. This can be used as a reaction to dodge any non-homing projectile/magic attack or melee attack. You may also travel through walls, obstacles and creatures with Blink.
