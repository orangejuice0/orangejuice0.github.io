Whenever you would apply a debuff to an enemy, additionally give the enemy disadvantage on its next damage roll. (This effect cannot be activated by itself)

Street Brawler

Uppercut

You may spend 1 CP to enhance your next attack. This attack applies Dazed to an enemy on their next turn.

Momentum

When you take a movement action or ability, empower your next unarmed strike to deal 2 bonus damage.

Adrenaline

On your turn or as a reaction, you may spend 0 CP. You may not be reduced below 1 HP until the start of your next turn. The cost of this ability increases by 1 CP each time you use it, resetting at the end of combat.

One-Sided Beatdown

Once per combat, you may select a Target. You may not attack any other creatures until the Target is incapacitated or dies. Your unarmed strikes against the Target apply damage modifiers twice.

Stance swapper

Madness/Flow

You may choose to enter Madness or Flow at the start of your turn. While in Madness, you suffer -2 on your attack rolls and -2 to your AC. However, your unarmed strikes deal double damage. While in Flow, you gain 1 DEX.

Vengeance/Counter

Upon taking damage from a melee attack, if you are in Madness, you may perform a free unarmed strike upon your aggressor. Upon avoiding a melee attack, if you are in Flow, you may perform a free unarmed strike upon your aggressor.

Leg Sweep

If you are in Flow, you may spend 2 CP to perform a Leg Sweep, tripping your target. The target takes one less action on its next turn, and its DEX no longer affects its AC until the start of its next turn.

River of Wrath

While in Madness, attacks with unarmed strikes no longer cost CP. You only may perform unarmed attacks twice per turn.

Cyclone

Your attacks with unarmed strikes now hit thrice, at 1D2 base damage per hit. Each hit applies on-hit effects and damage modifiers.
