Nature & Survival

Evolution

Survival of the Fittest

After unlocking this ability, when you level up, acquire the tier 1 Adaptation corresponding to the terrain you are in. (Refer to the Adaptation Table)

Wild Heart

Gain 2 Max HP each time you acquire an Adaptation (Also applies on previously gained Adaptations)

Survival of the Fittest+

After acquiring the tier 1 Adaptation for a certain type of terrain, levelling up in that same type of terrain grants you the tier 2 Adaptation.

Wild One

At the start of combat, gain an amount of Wildness equal to the number of Adaptations you have. At the start of each of your turns, you may choose to apply any of the following effects: Gain 2 bonus STR and lose 1 CHA; Gain 2 bonus DEX and lose 1 WIS; Gain 2 bonus CON and lose 1 INT; Gain 2 bonus WIS and lose 1 DEX; Gain 2 bonus INT and lose 1 CON; Gain 2 CHA and lose 1 STR. Each effect costs 1 Wildness to apply, lasts until the end of your turn, and can only be activated once per turn.

Transformation

Transformation

Every 12 hours out of daylight, gain 1 level of Fatigue.

Pyrophobia

Burning is permanent.

Loss of Senses

Starting with the first turn, at the start of every other turn in combat, inflict yourself with one Dazed.

Photosynthesize

Gain 1 additional CP at the start of every turn.

Oathsworn Companion

Oathsworn Companion

When you tame a non-sentient creature, you may swear an oath to it, designating it as your Oathsworn Companion. You may only have one Oathsworn Companion. Upon dying, your Oathsworn Companion dies and vice versa. Being resurrected resurrects your Oathsworn Companion and vice versa. You gain the ability to communicate with your companion telepathically up to 60FT. It understands you and vice versa. When you level up, your Oathsworn Companion does so too.

Unfaltering Trust

Whenever you would make a saving throw, your Oathsworn does too (and vice versa). If at least one succeeds, you succeed the saving throw.

Tag Team Tactics

If you and your Oathsworn Companion target the same enemy with attacks on the same turn, for the rest of combat, it takes 1 additional damage from your and your Oathsworn Companion’s attacks. This effect only applies once per enemy.

Eternal Bond

You and your Oathsworn Companion apply each others’ bonuses on top of your own. Whenever an ailment is applied to you, it is additionally applied to your Oathsworn Companion and vice versa. When you take damage or heal, your Oathsworn Companion has the same amount applied and vice versa.

Barangyi’s Blessing

Unlock this ability immediately when you invest skill points in a skill from the Nature and Survival tree. Gain effects corresponding to how many skills you have in the tree: 1 - Gain 1 bonus WIS permanently; 3 - Increase your race bonuses by 1; 5 - Immediately level up twice; 8 - Once per turn, heal yourself and another battling ally HP equal to your level.
