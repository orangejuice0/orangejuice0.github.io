The New Gods

Holith

(Hoe-lith)

Fire god with power to control the flames, heat and magma of worlds it has dominion over.

Larisin

(Lar-i(soft)-sin)

Ocean god with power over the seas, oceans, their currents and the ability to influence the creatures in it; only in worlds it has dominion over.

Drowlesh

(Drow-lesh)

Lake god with power over lakes and the ability to influence the creatures in it; only in worlds it has dominion over.

Hénahra

(heir-na-ra)

River god with power over rivers and the ability to influence the currents and the creatures in it; only in worlds it has dominion over.

Boldara

(Bowl-dar-a)

Earth god with power over rock, land and dirt; in worlds it has dominion over.

Affecra

(Aff-ec-ra)

Care god with power healthing, nurture and compassion; in worlds it has dominion over.

Dec’qui

(Deck-qwee)

War god with the ability to grant worriers luck and power during a mighty battle; in worlds it has dominion over.

Easifka

(Ease-if-ka)

Storms god, creating storms that annoy their twin Easol, as they battle for control; in worlds it has dominion over.

Easol

(Ease-Soul)

Weather god with power over most weather patterns, but is constantly plagued by his twin Easifka; in worlds it has dominion over.

Aulmalo

(Owl-mal-ow)

Construction god who guides great architects, as well as being the architect of all creatures on the world they come from; only has power in worlds it has dominion over.

Barangyi

(bar-ang-yi)

Wild god, controls the shape and structure of nature; in worlds it has dominion over.

Nashua

(nash-oo-a)

Sleep god with power over the mental sub realm of the unconscious; in worlds it has dominion over.

Eyhobeha

(eye-who-bee-ha)

Wisdom and study god they guide the great scholars and creative minds, creating a spread of knowledge and enlightenment; in worlds it has dominion over.

Alasa

(Al-as-a)

Travel and voyage god they help to navigate lost travellers and supposedly inspired the great ship builder; have power in worlds it has dominion over.

Jiska

(jisk-ah)

Family god they defend the families of the worlds they have dominion over.

The Sculptor

The creator of the world of the people of the new gods, has no presence in the world as they did not sculpt it.
