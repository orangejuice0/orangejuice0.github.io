Arcane Weaponry

Enhancement

Magical Attacks

You may choose to have your attacks with Arcane Weapons apply INT modifiers instead.

Mana-fuelled Strikes

Whenever you would perform a physical attack with an Arcane Weapon, you may spend 1 mana. If the attack is successful, deal bonus damage equal to your INT.

Mana-fuelled Strikes+

If a creature takes bonus damage from the effect of Mana-fuelled Strikes, decrease their INT by 1 and gain 1 bonus INT until the end of your next turn.

Magical Attacks+

Your attacks with Arcane Weapons now apply both their original modifiers and INT modifiers.

Spellweaving

Spellweaving

When you perform a successful attack using an Arcane Weapon, your next offensive spell has its Mana cost reduced by 1. This ability cannot be stacked.

Follow-up

When you successfully deal damage with an offensive spell, increase your next roll to hit using an Arcane Weapon by 1. This ability cannot be stacked.

At the end of your turns, if you have performed a successful attack using an Arcane Weapon and dealt damage with an offensive spell since the end of your last turn, gain the following ability: Once per round, until the start of your next turn, as a reaction to any enemy or allied action, you may choose to perform an attack using an Arcane weapon or offensive spell. If you perform a successful attack using an Arcane Weapon using this effect, regain 1 Mana. If you deal damage with an offensive spell using this effect, gain 1 bonus INT until the end of your next turn.

Arcane Assault

Once per combat, you may spend 1 CP to perform an Arcane Assault upon an enemy. Against that enemy: when you successfully cast a spell against that enemy, your next attack using an Arcane Weapon does not have a CP cost. When you perform a successful attack using an Arcane Weapon, your next spell has no CP cost. When you fail an attack or spell, immediately end your turn.

Error

Potency

When Overcrafting a potion, you may choose to double the Overcraft effect. Add another Overcraft Table effect to the potion.

Overenthusiasm

When brewing a potion, gain +20 to your dice roll

Fixing the odds

You may now select your Overcraft Table effects

Alchemical Mastery

When applying ailments with your potions, applying an ailment that a creature is already afflicted by now afflicts them with the respective enhanced ailment
