Arcane Weaponry

Enhancement

Magical Attacks

You may choose to have your attacks with Arcane Weapons apply INT modifiers instead.

Mana-fuelled Strikes

Whenever you would perform a physical attack with an Arcane Weapon, you may spend 1 mana. If the attack is successful, deal bonus damage equal to your INT.

Mana-fuelled Strikes+

If a creature takes bonus damage from the effect of Mana-fuelled Strikes, decrease their INT by 1 and gain 1 bonus INT until the end of your next turn.

Magical Attacks+

Your attacks with Arcane Weapons now apply both their original modifiers and INT modifiers.

Spellweaving

Spellweaving

When you perform a successful attack using an Arcane Weapon, your next offensive spell has its Mana cost reduced by 1. This ability cannot be stacked.

Follow-up

When you successfully deal damage with an offensive spell, increase your next roll to hit using an Arcane Weapon by 1. This ability cannot be stacked.

At the end of your turns, if you have performed a successful attack using an Arcane Weapon and dealt damage with an offensive spell since the end of your last turn, gain the following ability: Once per round, until the start of your next turn, as a reaction to any enemy or allied action, you may choose to perform an attack using an Arcane weapon or offensive spell. If you perform a successful attack using an Arcane Weapon using this effect, regain 1 Mana. If you deal damage with an offensive spell using this effect, gain 1 bonus INT until the end of your next turn.

Arcane Assault

Once per combat, you may spend 1 CP to perform an Arcane Assault upon an enemy. Against that enemy: when you successfully cast a spell against that enemy, your next attack using an Arcane Weapon does not have a CP cost. When you perform a successful attack using an Arcane Weapon, your next spell has no CP cost. When you fail an attack or spell, immediately end your turn.

Soulbound Weapons

Soulbound Weapon

You may only unlock this ability after you have slain 100 enemies using one type of Arcane Weapon (You do not need to have landed the killing blow). Become Soulbound to that Arcane Weapon (It still qualifies as an Arcane Weapon). During your turns, you may spend 1 CP to manifest your Soulbound Weapon without a Mana cost. You may choose to dematerialise your Soulbound Weapon at any time (This does not have a CP cost). Being disarmed automatically dematerialises the weapon. You may only manifest one Soulbound Weapon at a time.

Spiritual Recovery

When you manifest your Soulbound Weapon, until the end of your next turn, gain 1 Mana each time you perform a successful attack with your Soulbound Weapon.

Synchronisation

You may only unlock this ability after you have slain 300 enemies with your Soulbound Weapon. When you have performed 5 successful attacks with your Soulbound Weapon, at the start of your turns, if you are not Dazed, you may choose to Synchronise with your weapon. While Synchronised, gain 10FT movement. You gain +10 to hit while using your Soulbound Weapon. While Synchronised, if you take damage, immediately end Synchronisation. You are inflicted with 3 turns of Dazed.

Evolved Weapon

You may only unlock this ability after you have slain 500 enemies with your Soulbound Weapon. Your Soulbound Weapon becomes an Evolved Weapon.

Relearning

You may invest in two more branches from this tree. You can learn the same branch twice with this effect. Relearning the same branch doubles the effects of the abilities in that branch. You must still pay SP costs and meet any prerequisites needed to unlock the ability
