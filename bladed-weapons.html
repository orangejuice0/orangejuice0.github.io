Bladed Weapons

Axe

Cleave

Your axe attacks now hit all enemies within its attack range. Your attacks heal you for 1 HP for each enemy hit.

Blood Rite

Once per turn, you may spend 12 HP. Gain 1 CP and 1 bonus STR for the rest of combat.

Feast

Upon killing an enemy, heal for 3 HP and gain 1 bonus STR for the rest of combat (max 3)

Reaper

You may spend 1 CP: Until the end of your turn, Increase incoming healing from all sources by your bonus STR.

Sword

Skilled Swordplay

Every third successful sword strike on any one enemy will always critically strike.

En Garde

After a critical strike, your next attack on the same target will always hit.

Parry

When an enemy would deal melee physical damage to you - as a reaction, you may spend 2 CP. Any damage that would’ve been dealt to you is dealt to them as a critical strike.

Virtuosity

Your critical strikes deal X+1 times base damage, where X is the amount of your successful critical strikes to your target this combat.

Spear & Pike

Lunge

Your spear attacks now have a range of 15FT. Your attacks at maximum range deal an additional 3 damage.

Hold Your Ground

Gain advantage to hit on spear attacks at maximum range

Halt

If an enemy would move inside or into your spear’s attack range, you may choose to immediately perform a spear attack upon them (this still costs 1 CP). If this attack is successful, end their movement action.

Ultimatum

You may spend 2 CP - attack every enemy in your range. Enemies hit by this attack are pushed away to your maximum range.

Bladesurge

Once per turn, before you attack an enemy - if you have already attacked this turn, you may immediately perform an additional attack. This additional attack copies any bonuses applied to the initial attack.
