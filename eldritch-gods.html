The Eldritch Gods

Silence

The eldritch lord of absolute, complete nothingness. It twists the mind and corrupts the spirit until all that is left is either dead, mad or completely subservient.

Cuth'Sarogg

(cuff-tsar-ogg)

The eldritch lord of Mindlessness, a focus on twisting the mind of those unaware of its true intentions. Creates terrorists and madmen to help create Chaos

Yogsedrig'os

(Yog-said-rig-os)

The eldritch lord of Chaos, uses Cuth'Sarogg actions to create Chaos, confusion and fear as well as forming illusions and controlling animals to act strangely.

Nyesig-ungores

(knee-air-sig-un-gore-es)

The eldritch lord of Devastation will destroy world's that have been corrupted, taking its matter and power for itself along with that of its gods.

Cthusunig

(cthu-soon-ig)

The eldritch lord of Corruption, it makes key parts of worlds into the opposite of themselves and their purpose, disbalancing the world.

Nzeleph

(n-zel-ef)

The eldritch lord of False visions, shows heroes, the devout and adventurers false visions of the future in an attempt to confuse them or to make them betray their cause.

Azheh-alog

(a-zeh-a-log)

Ruler of the legion: leads the armies of the eldritch, those twisted by Cthusunig or that have given up themselves to the power willingly.
