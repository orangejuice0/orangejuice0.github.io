The Old Gods

Embris

(b(soft)-r(soft)is-ing)

Fire god with the power to control flames and heat of the worlds they have dominion over.

Aquala

(aqua-la)

Water and ice god with the power to control the water and ice of the worlds they have dominion over.

Tempris

(Tem-pris)

Air and storms god with the power to control the weather of the worlds they have dominion over.

Coltostam

(cohl-tos-tam)

Harvest god with the power to bless harvests and farmers during harvest time, of the worlds they have dominion over.

Dyria

(Deer-ee-a)

Beast god with power over all beasts in the worlds they have dominion over.

Dragael

(drag-ail)

Dragon god with power over dragons in the worlds they have dominion over.

Uhyrstro

(oo-hears-trow)

Monster god with power over monsters in the worlds they have dominion over.

Terranor

(tear-a-nor)

Earth god with power over land, rock and dirt of the worlds they have dominion over.

Ordael

(or-dale)

Order god with the ability to encourage order in societies, also known as the god of monarchs and rulers. Power in the worlds they have dominion over.

Sprigtra

(Sprig-t-ra)

Sprite god with power over sprites in the worlds they have dominion over.

Giganantis

(Giga-nan-tis)

Giant god with power over Giants, goliaths, trolls and titans in the worlds they have dominion over.

Blokil

(Block-ill)

War god with the desire for glorious victory through sheer determination. He is the offspring of one of the great ‘lord killers’.
