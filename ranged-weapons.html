Ranged Weapons

Crossbow

Quickshot

Once per combat, you may load a non-lethal shot into your crossbow with no CP cost. This shot cannot reduce an enemy below 1 HP and damage modifiers do not apply. If an enemy reaches 1 HP from this ability, they are disabled for the rest of combat.

Fast Fingers

You may now fire your crossbow as a reaction to any allied or enemy action.

Quickload

You may choose to reload your crossbow whenever you fire it. (this still has a CP cost)

Multi Bolt

Once per turn, you are able to enhance a crossbow attack. Fire two bolts at your enemy instead of one (these count as separate attacks).

Bow

Lock On

Once per combat, you may select a Target. You gain +2 to attack rolls against the Target.

Pin

Once per combat, you may enhance your attack to displace a Target 10FT. If they are knocked into an obstacle, pin them to it until the end of their next turn. (When pinned, an enemy has disadvantage on attack rolls and cannot move)

Deadshot

You may spend 2 CP to take a precise shot at a Target’s weak point. This shot always critically strikes and applies ailments based on the type of weak point hit.

Sharpsight

Your arrows deal additional damage based on the distance travelled (1 bonus damage for every 50FT distance)

Thrown

Hinder

Throwing projectiles at enemies will embed the projectile in them. While projectiles are embedded in an enemy, they are Hindered, halving their movement

Lightning Reflexes

You may spend 1 CP to catch an incoming projectile..

Recall

Once per combat, you may spend 1 CP to recall all thrown weapons (within 50FT of you) that you’ve thrown back to you. Weapons will travel around objects and creatures. However, if a weapon is already embedded in an enemy, it deals damage again (this does not apply damage modifiers) and additionally applies Bleeding to the enemy.

Recall+

Recalled weapons now apply damage modifiers

Flawless Form

Once per combat, at the start of your turn, you may activate this effect: Your ranged attacks cannot miss and always critically strike.
