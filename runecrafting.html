Runecrafting

Summoning

Rune of Construction

When this rune is resolved, summon a Rune Golem. The Rune Golem enqueues the cycle resolving this rune. When the Rune Golem resolves Rune of Construction, it instead attacks an enemy. After its cycle ends, the Rune Golem is destroyed.

Rune of Accuracy

When this rune is resolved, all existing allied Rune Golems gain +1 to hit on their next attack.

Rune of Augmentation

When this rune is resolved, grant all existing allied Rune Golems an additional effect: When you perform a successful attack on a battling enemy, do not become destroyed at the end of your cycle. Enqueue your previous cycle after your cycle ends.

Rune of Enchantment

When this rune is resolved, your next successful attack deals bonus PMD equal to the number of runes that have been resolved this combat.

Manipulation

Rune of Acceleration

When this rune is resolved, immediately resolve the next rune in the cycle. This rune has no effect if it is the last rune in a cycle.

Rune of Duplication

When this rune is enqueued, destroy this rune. Replace it with the next rune in the cycle. This rune has no effect if it is the last rune in a cycle.

Rune of Recycling

When this rune is resolved, enqueue your current cycle into a simultaneous sub-cycle. If the sub-cycle contains Rune of Cycling, replace it with Rune of Acceleration.

Rune of Extension

When this rune is enqueued, add two rune slots to the end of the cycle. When this rune resolves, transform it into a Rune of Acceleration.

Output

Rune of Harm

When this rune is resolved, the nearest battling enemy takes 1D4 PMD. This damage repeats for each time Rune of Harm has resolved this combat.

Rune of Protection

When this rune is resolved, until the end of your turn, gain Temporary HP equal to 5x your INT.

Rune of Reflection

When this rune is resolved, gain the following effect until the start of your next turn: As a reaction, when an enemy creature targets you with an ability or attack, you can force them to make an INT saving throw against you. Upon failing this throw, negate that ability or attack. Any effects/damage that would be inflicted upon you by their action is instead inflicted onto them. This rune can only be enqueued in the final rune slot of a cycle, and if Rune of Acceleration would resolve this rune, it instead has no effect.

Rune of Destruction

When this rune is enqueued, resolve the effect of Rune of Harm. When this rune is resolved, destroy all remaining runes in the cycle. Enqueue each empty slot in the cycle with Rune of Destruction.

Rune of Ascension

When this rune is resolved, if you are a Rune Golem, become invulnerable to damage. Otherwise, gain the following effect: enqueuing a Main Cycle no longer has a CP cost, and you may now enqueue the same rune twice in a cycle.
